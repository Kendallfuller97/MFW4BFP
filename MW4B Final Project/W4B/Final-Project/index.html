Animal Facts That You Probably Didn't Know

Elephants are very afraid of bees. Farmers even use beehives to deter Elephants from coming onto their land.
During Hibernation (which is 5-7 months) Bears don’t go to the bathroom. They are actually able to turn excess urea (a component of urine) back into protein, which means that they actually gain muscle while hibernating.
Did you know Cheetahs have built-in "sunglasses"Much like the Cheetos mascot, Cheetahs have natural sunglasses that protect their eyes. This comes in the form of two black lines running from their eyes to their mouth. The cheetah’s tear line helps reflect the sun’s glare as cheetahs hunt in the arid savannas of their habitats. How’s that for amazing cheetah facts?
Monkeys are found almost everywhere on Earth, except for Australia and Antarctica
